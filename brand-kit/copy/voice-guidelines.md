# YorkiExchange Voice & Tone Guidelines

## Brand Voice

YorkiExchange communicates with a voice that is:

### Friendly
- Warm and welcoming without being overly casual
- Approachable language that puts users at ease
- Conversational but never unprofessional

### Clear
- Direct and concise communication
- No jargon, buzzwords, or corporate speak
- Simple language that anyone can understand

### Community-First
- Emphasis on peer-to-peer connections
- "We" language that includes the user in the community
- Focus on shared values and trust

### Responsible
- Serious about breeding ethics and pet welfare
- Thoughtful about the gravity of pet ownership decisions
- Factual and educational when appropriate

---

## What We Are NOT

❌ **Baby talk or excessive cuteness**
- No "pupperino," "doggo," "smol bean," etc.
- No excessive emojis or decorative language
- Yorkies are dogs, not cartoon characters

❌ **Corporate or salesy**
- No "leverage," "synergy," "ecosystem," etc.
- No pressure tactics or artificial urgency
- No exaggerated claims or hype

❌ **Casual or irreverent**
- This is about living creatures and responsible ownership
- Breeding and rehoming are serious decisions
- Maintain respect for the process and the animals

## Grammar & Style

### Capitalization
- **Brand name**: Always "YorkiExchange" (CamelCase)
- **Headlines**: Sentence case preferred ("Find your perfect Yorkie")
- **Buttons/CTAs**: Title case or uppercase ("Contact Seller" or "CONTACT SELLER")

### Punctuation
- Use periods for full sentences
- Skip periods in button labels and short UI text
- Use exclamation points sparingly — only for genuine excitement
- Oxford comma: yes ("health, temperament, and lineage")

### Numbers
- Spell out one through nine ("three Yorkies available")
- Use numerals for 10+ ("12 listings match your search")
- Use numerals for measurements ("8 weeks old," "5 lbs")
- Use numerals with units ("$1,500," "3 years old")

### Abbreviations
- Spell out on first use, then abbreviate if necessary
- Common: AKC (American Kennel Club), DOB (date of birth), lbs (pounds)
- Avoid text-speak (no "u," "r," "btw")

## Writing Patterns

### Active Voice
✅ "Contact the seller to ask questions."
❌ "Questions can be asked by contacting the seller."

### Positive Framing
✅ "Complete your profile to unlock messaging."
❌ "You can't message sellers until you complete your profile."

### User-Focused
✅ "Your listing is live!"
❌ "The system has published your listing."

### Specific Over Generic
✅ "Response rate: Replies within 2 hours"
❌ "Fast response time"

---

## Empathy Guidelines

### Breeding & Adoption
- Acknowledge that choosing a Yorkie is a significant decision
- Recognize emotional investment in finding the right match
- Respect both breeders and adopters as responsible stewards

### Rehoming
- Never use judgmental language about rehoming
- Frame rehoming as responsible when circumstances change
- Emphasize finding the best outcome for the dog

### Community Moderation
- Be firm but respectful when enforcing rules
- Explain *why* a rule exists, not just *what* it is
- Assume good intent; guide users toward better behavior

- "Responsible breeding"
- "Verified breeder"
- "Connect with confidence"
- "Find your perfect Yorkie"
- "Rehome responsibly"
- "Built by Yorkie people, for Yorkie people"

---

## Avoid These Phrases

- "Amazing deal" / "Don't miss out" (pressure tactics)
- "Guaranteed" / "Perfect match" (unrealistic promises)
- "Premium puppies" / "Luxury Yorkies" (commodifying animals)
- "Act now" / "Limited time" (artificial urgency)
- "Must-have" / "Game-changer" (hype/buzzwords)

---

## Accessibility & Inclusivity

### Plain Language
- Write at an 8th-grade reading level for body text
- Define technical terms when necessary (e.g., "dam" = mother dog)

### Inclusive Language
- Avoid gendered language when unnecessary ("owner" not "mom/dad")
- Use "they/them" for unknown individuals
- Recognize diverse family structures and living situations

### Screen Reader Friendly
- Write descriptive alt text for images
- Use heading hierarchy properly
- Avoid relying solely on color to convey meaning

- ❌ "Nothing here."

### Error Messages
- ✅ "We couldn't load this page. Check your connection and try again."
- ❌ "Error 404: Resource not found."

### Success Messages
- ✅ "Message sent! You'll be notified when the seller replies."
- ❌ "Operation completed successfully."

## Voice Checklist

Before publishing any copy, ask:

- [ ] Is this clear and easy to understand?
- [ ] Does it sound like a real person, not a robot?
- [ ] Is it respectful of both animals and people?
- [ ] Does it avoid jargon, hype, and pressure tactics?
- [ ] Is the spelling of "YorkiExchange" correct?
- [ ] Would I say this out loud to a friend?
